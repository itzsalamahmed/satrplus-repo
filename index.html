Jaflong Tourist Spot

People Comes Here To Refresh There mind

Situated at the border between Bangladesh and Indian state Meghalaya, Jaflong is one of the most Tourist Attractions in Jaflong destinations in Sylhet. Overshadowed by subtropical mountains and rain-forests, this place is about 60 km from Sylhet town. This scenic spot is situated on the river Piain. Its landscape beauty among gardens and hills enchants the tourists. The Mari River coming from the Himalayan Mountain range brings tons of stone boulders with its stream.
